General functions supported by the FML-NSpire:

sin(x) - in radians
cos(x) - in radians
tan(x) - in radians
ln(x) - natural logarithm
sqrt(x) - square root
neg(x) - returns the negative of x (use this instead of the minus sign)

General constants supported by the FML-NSpire:

e - Euler's number, approximately equal to 2.71828
pi - approximately equal to 3.14159
ans - the previous value calculated
